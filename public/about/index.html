About MmSpicy
io

MmSpicy io is a fun, fast paced, multiplayer game that's easy to join and play. We started off with the tag game mode, then we added the laser tag game mode. We plan on adding more game modes in the future. This takes time though, as we are a small team of developers.

The project was created to help players explore coding and game design in a hands-on, engaging way using the A-Frame Library. We believe learning is more fun when it’s interactive—and a little spicy.

We're always adding new features, most of them inspired by our supportive community of players. Whether it's suggesting cool new skins, maps, or maybe even new game modes, our players shape the game. Got an idea, or just want to chat? Contact us here.
